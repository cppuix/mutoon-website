Al-Bayquniyyah — parallel columns
أَلْحَمْدُ لِلهِ مُصَلِّيًا عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ خَيْرِ نَبِيٍّ أُرْسِلَا
وَذِي مِنْ أَقْسَامِ الحَدِيثِ عِدَّةٌ وَكُلٌّ وَاجِدٍ آتِي وَحَدَّهٌ
Praise is due to Allah, while sending blessings upon Muhammad, the best of Prophets sent forth.
And this poem consists of a number of categories of Hadith, and each one is coming with its definition.
